What is Data Science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that involves using statistical and computational methods to extract insights and knowledge from data. It encompasses various techniques, including data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization, to extract meaningful information and insights from complex and large datasets.

Why is data science important?

It extract valuable insights and knowledge from this data that can be used to improve decision-making, drive innovation, and solve complex problems in various fields. By applying statistical and computational methods to analyze and interpret data, data scientists can identify patterns, trends, and correlations that can help us make predictions about future outcomes. Additionally, data science is crucial for developing intelligent machines and algorithms, which can automate tasks and improve efficiency in various industries. Overall, data science has become an essential tool for businesses, researchers, and policymakers to make informed decisions and create positive impact in society.
